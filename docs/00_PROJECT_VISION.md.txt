# 1. Executive Summary

The Smart Textile Business Management & AI-Powered E-Commerce Platform is a comprehensive digital transformation solution designed for textile retailers and uniform manufacturers. The platform integrates online commerce, inventory management, production tracking, customer relationship management, payment processing, and artificial intelligence into a single, scalable system.

Unlike traditional textile management software that focuses only on inventory or sales, this platform provides complete business process automation from customer order placement to product delivery while supporting intelligent business decision-making through predictive analytics.

The system is designed as a production-ready web application using modern software engineering principles, modular architecture, and cloud-ready technologies.

---

# 2. Project Background

Many small and medium textile businesses in Sri Lanka still rely on manual workflows including paper records, spreadsheets, phone calls, and messaging applications for handling customer orders, inventory, and production.

These traditional processes create numerous operational challenges including:

* Slow order processing
* Inventory inaccuracies
* Production delays
* Poor customer experience
* Limited online presence
* Lack of business analytics
* Reactive decision making

As customer expectations continue to increase and digital commerce becomes the industry standard, businesses require integrated software solutions capable of managing both retail and manufacturing operations efficiently.

# 12. High-Level System Overview

The system consists of four primary layers:

1. **Presentation Layer** – Customer website and administrative dashboard.
2. **Application Layer** – Backend services handling business logic, authentication, APIs, payments, and production workflows.
3. **Data Layer** – PostgreSQL database, caching, and file storage.
4. **Intelligence Layer** – AI services for forecasting, recommendations, analytics, and business insights.

Each layer is designed to be modular, scalable, and independently maintainable.
